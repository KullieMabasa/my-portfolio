The Weather Apps

This app allows users to easily check the current weather conditions for any city they desire. I built it using a combination of technologies, including AJAX, APIs, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS.

  • City Search: Users can input the name of their desired city in the search bar.
  • Real-Time Data: The app fetches real-time weather data using AJAX from external APIs, ensuring accuracy and up-to-date information
  • Weather Details: Once users enter a city, the app displays essential weather details, including temperature, wind speed, and humidity.
  • Units Conversion: Users have the flexibility to view temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit, wind speed in km/h or mph, and humidity as a percentage.
  • Responsive Design: The app's layout is designed using CSS to ensure responsiveness across all devices, including desktops, laptops, tablets, and mobile phones.

The Driving School

This website serves as an online platform to showcase the driving school in my area, providing essential information and images to potential clients. The main goal of this project is to enable clients to view the available driving lesson packages and easily book their preferred lessons. To create this website, I utilized HTML and CSS, along with external resources such as Google Fonts and icons from external websites.

  • Information and Images: The website presents detailed information about the driving school's services, instructors, and facilities. It incorporates high-quality images to give visitors a glimpse of the school's environment.
  • Driving Lesson Packages: The website lists the various driving lesson packages available, including details on each package's duration, cost, and what it covers.
  • Booking Functionality: To facilitate seamless booking, the website offers an interactive form that allows clients to select their preferred driving lesson package, provide their contact details, and book a suitable time slot.
  • Google Maps Integration: For easy navigation, the website embeds a Google Maps iframe, enabling visitors to locate the driving school's physical address easily.
  • Styling with Google Fonts and Icons: To enhance the website's appearance, I used Google Fonts to select attractive and readable typography. Additionally, I incorporated icons from external websites to add visual elements and improve the overall user experience.
